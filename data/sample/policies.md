# Company Policies

## Refund Policy

Customers may request a full refund within **30 days** of purchase if the product has not been substantially used. Refund requests must include the order ID and reason. Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method within **5–10 business days**. Subscription refunds are prorated for the unused portion of the billing period.

Partial refunds may apply when more than 50% of included usage quotas have been consumed. Enterprise contracts follow the terms in the signed agreement.

## Shipping Policy

Physical kits ship within **2 business days** after payment confirmation. Standard delivery takes 3–7 business days domestically and 7–14 days internationally. Tracking numbers are emailed when the order leaves the warehouse.

Express shipping is available at checkout for next-business-day delivery in supported regions. We do not ship to PO boxes for hardware bundles requiring signature confirmation.

## Returns

Return merchandise within 14 days in original packaging. Opened software licenses are non-returnable once activation keys are delivered. Contact support to receive a return authorization (RMA) number before sending items back.

## Privacy

Support tickets may contain personal data processed under our Privacy Policy. Agents access only data required to resolve the ticket. Ticket content is retained for 24 months for quality and compliance purposes.

## Escalation

Unresolved issues after two support interactions can be escalated to a senior agent. Escalations are reviewed within 4 business hours on business days.
