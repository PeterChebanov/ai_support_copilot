# Internal Admin Compensation Policy

This document is restricted to the `admin` role.

## Salary bands

Junior support agents: **€38,000-€46,000** annual gross salary.
Senior support agents: **€52,000-€68,000** annual gross salary.

## Admin-only review policy

Compensation changes require approval from the support director and finance partner.
Managers must not disclose salary bands in public or customer-facing channels.
