# Support FAQ

## Account & Login

### How do I reset my password?

Go to the login page and click **Forgot password**. Enter your work email. You will receive a reset link within 5 minutes. Links expire after 24 hours.

### How do I change my email address?

Open **Settings → Profile** and update your email. You must confirm the new address before it becomes active.

### Why am I locked out after failed login attempts?

After five failed attempts in 15 minutes, the account is locked for 30 minutes. Contact support if you need an immediate unlock.

## Orders & Billing

### Where can I see my invoices?

Invoices are available under **Billing → Invoices**. You can download PDF copies for the last 24 months.

### How do I update payment method?

Go to **Billing → Payment methods**, add a new card, and set it as default. Old cards can be removed after the new one is verified.

## Product Usage

### How do I invite a teammate?

Admins can invite users from **Team → Members → Invite**. Invited users receive an email with a 7-day activation link.

### What browsers are supported?

We support the latest two versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Internet Explorer is not supported.

### How do I export my data?

Use **Settings → Data export**. Exports are generated within 24 hours and sent to your registered email as a secure download link.

## Support Hours

### When is live chat available?

Live chat is available Monday–Friday, 9:00–18:00 CET. Outside these hours, submit a ticket and we respond within one business day.

### How fast do you respond to tickets?

Priority 1 tickets: 4 business hours. Priority 2: 1 business day. Priority 3: 2 business days.

## Security

### Do you support SSO?

Yes. SAML and OIDC SSO are available on Pro and Enterprise plans. Configure SSO under **Security → Single sign-on**.

### How do I report a security issue?

Email security@example.com with reproduction steps. Do not post vulnerabilities in public tickets.
